Our Due Diligence Charter



Introduction

Our Deal Page contains the core proposition, information about the issuer’s business and important disclosure documents. All investors must read our deal page carefully as it outlines important information on the business, its value proposition and terms of investment offering.

You must also consider your own personal investment requirements and consider making further due diligence. Please reach out to us should you have any further questions regarding the ongoing campaign and/or investment proposition.

Ata Plus Sdn Bhd is an Equity Crowdfunding platform and is a registered Recognised Market Operator (RMO) with the Securities Commission Malaysia (SC) that approves ECF campaigns in accordance with the requirements of the SC RMO Guidelines and the Capital Market Services Act related laws and regulations and SC Guidelines. Ata Plus is not an adviser and does not make a recommendation on the investment proposition on the platform and does not tailor due diligence to individual requirements.

Investors should be aware that Ata Plus relies upon the information provided by the issuer, who are required and responsible to ensure all the information provided is true and correct. Ata Plus also uses third parties service providers and tools to conduct due diligence. Ata Plus’ investor terms and conditions, including Ata Plus’ limitation of liability, apply to investments made on the site and can be found here.

Verification of Information

Item Description

Valuation

Ata Plus may provide inputs and guidance to the Issuer, but the decision on what valuation to offer is totally at the Issuer’s discretion. Typically, the issuer would have to provide the basis on how the valuation is derived.

Factual Claims

Ata Plus ensures that all statements in Deal Page, Company Updates posted on Deal Page are accurate. This verification process involves obtaining and reviewing supporting documentation or independent evidence where applicable. Examples of supporting documentation are contracts, leases, licenses, certification, achievements/recognition/awards received, academic certificates, patents and the like.

Aspirational Statements and Forward looking Statement, including stated use of funds

Ata Plus verifies and ensures the alignment between the business plan and the financial projections. This includes assessing the assumptions used for future projects, plans, and intended use of funds to match the business targets.

Tax Relief

With reference to email sent by Zue, Ata Plus will ensure accurate investor information is provided to the SC for verification. Once Ata Plus receives the verification from SC, Ata Plus will then forward the information to verified investors.

Rewards

Ata Plus will assess if the rewards offered to investors as a value added benefit in the investment offer are reasonable and feasible.

Financial Information

Item Description

Historical Financial

Ata Plus will assess Audited Financial Statements and verify with the company and/or third party service providers where required. Submission of the latest Audited Financial Statement is compulsory under Section 13.24(e) under the RMO Guidelines.

Risks

Item Description

Risk Disclosure

Where it warrants key risk information needs to be highlighted, this information is prominently featured at the top of the Campaign Page.

Legal

Item Description

Corporate

Ata Plus will review the corporate structure of the Company. This assessment encompasses the composition of shareholders, group organization and subsidiaries. Ata Plus will scrutinize constitutional documents such as articles of association and any shareholders' agreements, with a specific focus on examining the ECF investors, protection.

In instances where there are different share classes with different rights, we will provide disclosure along with details of how these differing rights might affect the ECF investor/shareholder.

Litigation

Ata Plus conducts inquiries regarding any litigation or disputes involving the Issuer and conducts searches to verify any court judgments against the Issuer. Upon disclosure of potential litigation, Ata Plus will conduct further inquiries, particularly regarding the potential financial implications and the risk of the Issuer losing the litigation. Ata Plus will prominently feature details of such litigation at the top of the Campaign Page. However, if the Issuer is involved in material litigation that Ata Plus believes could significantly impact the business, Ata Plus will not proceed with listing a Campaign until the litigation is resolved.

The Company & Key Personnel

Item Description

Background Checks

Ata Plus will perform background checks on the company and its directors, which will include personal credit and bankruptcy checks, director's disqualification checks, previous company checks, and accreditation checks.

Key Personnel

Ata Plus will ensure that there is a suitable level of full-time commitment, relevant experience and/or background related to the business from Key Personnel.

Ata Plus will verify the information provided on the experience, qualifications and Honorary Titles received.
